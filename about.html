About Us

The Nelson College Environmental Committee was started in 2015 with one simple goal: to increase the biodiversity of Nelson College and the surrounding Grampians. Although a very big goal, it has been worked towards by planting various species of New Zealand native flora over the years since then.

Beyond 2015, the committee has expanded into the many different branches of sustainability and environmental action. It has started a recycling initiative and is looking at starting pest control and composting initiatives in 2024. Committee members have also taken part in many different environmental strikes, gatherings, and cross-school collaborations to not only increase the college's environmental awareness and sustainability but that of the wider community as well.

The Nelson College Environmental Committee is a future-focused committee. We focus on ensuring that the environment is nurtured not only in the present but also in the future. We make sure that Nelson College maintains sustainable practices to minimise our impact on the environment and to improve the environmental awareness of our students. The Nelson College Environmental Committee is necessary in ensuring that the students of Nelson College are aware of their impact and take environmental action to reduce not only their own, but that of others. Spreading further awareness, and further action.
