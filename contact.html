If you want to create meaningful change within Nelson College and the wider community, join the Nelson College Environmental Committee.

Meetings are held every Tuesday lunchtime in L9 in which we do various activities, such as tree planting, mulching, organising events, or planning for future initiatives.

If you have any questions, concerns, or ideas, do not hesitate to come along and ask! Alternatively, feel free to contact us via email or Teams:
